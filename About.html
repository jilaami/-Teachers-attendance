About

This web-based QR code attendance system was developed as an alternative method for monitoring teachers’ attendance. Unlike fingerprint-based systems, this solution uses unique QR codes generated by teachers for faster and more accessible attendance tracking. The system was created to allow the Teachers to test and evaluate a more flexible and convenient approach to attendance monitoring. By simply scanning a QR code, attendance can be recorded instantly without the need for biometric devices. This platform reduces manual recording, minimizes errors, and saves time. It is designed to provide a simple, organized, and efficient way of managing teachers’ daily attendance.
